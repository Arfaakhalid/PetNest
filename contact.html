Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PetNest Network

Simply create an account as an adopter, browse available pets, and use our chat system to connect with the current owner or shelter. Once you find a match, you can arrange a meeting and complete the adoption process.

PetNest is free for adopters, reporters, and shelters. Sellers and pet accessory stores pay a small commission on successful transactions to help maintain and improve our platform.

All shelters and rescue organizations undergo a thorough verification process by our admin team before they can join PetNest. We check credentials, facilities, and track records to ensure they meet our standards for animal care.
